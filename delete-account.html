Account deletion

Request permanent deletion of your StallVantage account

This page lets a StallVantage user verify their identity, review what happens next, and then submit a final deletion request.

Deleting an account permanently removes access to StallVantage. Some records may be retained where law permits or requires retention, such as fraud prevention, security, tax, or compliance records.

Delete your account

To protect your data, we ask you to sign in first. After we verify your credentials, you will need to confirm the deletion one more time before anything is removed.

Final confirmation

Your account has been verified for your account. Confirm below to request permanent deletion.

  • Your account access will end after deletion is completed.
  • Associated user data will be removed through our secure backend deletion workflow.
  • Some information may be retained where legally required or reasonably necessary for fraud prevention, security, or recordkeeping.
Enter your email and password to verify your account before continuing.

Deletion summary

After your account is deleted, this page will show a summary of the data and files removed.

How this works

1
Enter the email and password for the StallVantage account you want to delete.
2
If the credentials are valid, this page unlocks a separate final confirmation step.
3
After final confirmation, the page calls a protected backend deletion function that removes account data and deletes the account.

Need help instead?

If you cannot access your account or need assistance with a deletion request, contact contact@stallvantage.com.
