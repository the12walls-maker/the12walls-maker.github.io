System overview

Everything stays connected

Inventory, orders, prep, and analytics feed each other so you always have a current answer to the question that matters most: what should I make for the next market?

Inventory → capacity

Every inventory change updates how many items you can produce right now, without a separate calculation step.

Orders → prep

Customer orders become production tasks automatically, so custom work and market stock live in the same workflow.

Prep → inventory

Mark a batch complete and ingredient usage is reflected immediately, without duplicate entry.

Key capabilities

Built for the way market vendors work

StallVantage is designed around the operational rhythm of small-batch businesses: prep before the event, sell through the day, then understand what happened clearly afterward.

Event-based forecasting

Recommendations are based on the specific market, using your own history instead of a generic average.

Offline first

Use the app at outdoor markets and pop-ups with unreliable reception. Sync can wait until service returns.

Language that matches the work

Recipes, materials, builds, and batches feel closer to how makers think than a generic retail back office.

Market-by-market visibility

Compare which events are performing best, where margins are strongest, and what products move fastest.

Prep planning before the rush

Build a realistic production plan before the market instead of scrambling through notes and spreadsheets.

One workflow instead of five

Inventory, prep, and reporting stay tied together so the business stays easier to run week after week.
