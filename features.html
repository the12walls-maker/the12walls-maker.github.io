System overview

Designed for the rhythm of market work

StallVantage connects inventory, prep, orders, and market reporting so the business is easier to run before the event, during setup, and after the day is over.

Inventory → capacity

Every inventory change updates how many items you can make right now, without a second spreadsheet or extra calculation.

Orders → prep

Customer orders become production tasks automatically, so custom work and stall stock stay in the same workflow.

Prep → inventory

Mark work complete and the ingredients or materials used are reflected immediately.
