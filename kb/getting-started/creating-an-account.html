Getting Started

Creating an Account

StallVantage requires a free account to save your data across devices and to enable cloud sync.

Register

  1. Open StallVantage and tap Register on the welcome screen.
  2. Enter your email address and choose a password (minimum 8 characters).
  3. Tap Create Account.
  4. Check your email for a verification link and tap it to confirm your address.
  5. Return to the app — you will be taken directly into the onboarding wizard.

Sign in to an existing account

  1. Open StallVantage and tap Sign In.
  2. Enter your registered email and password.
  3. Tap Sign In.

If you forget your password, tap Forgot password? on the sign-in screen. A reset link will be sent to your email address.

Joining a team workspace

If a workspace owner has invited you to join their business:

  1. You will receive an invite code by email or from your owner directly.
  2. Open StallVantage, sign in (or register if you are new), and go to Settings → Team.
  3. Tap Join Workspace and enter the invite code.
  4. Your account is now linked to that workspace and will reflect the owner's subscription tier.

> Note: Team members cannot purchase subscriptions themselves. Subscription benefits are provided by the workspace owner.

Signing out

Go to Settings → Account and tap Sign Out. Your local data remains on the device until you clear the app.
