Pricing

Plan Price
Free $0 forever
Pro $9.99 / month
Business $24.99 / month or $249 / year
Ad Removal One-time purchase (removes ads from the Free plan)

Prices are in USD. Taxes and App Store / Google Play fees may apply depending on your region.

How to upgrade

  1. Go to Settings → Subscription.
  2. Tap the plan you want to switch to.
  3. Complete the purchase through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android).

Subscriptions renew automatically. You can cancel at any time through your App Store or Google Play account — your current-period access continues until the renewal date.

Team subscriptions

If you are the workspace owner on the Business plan, all team members you invite automatically receive Pro-level access at no extra charge. Team members do not need to purchase their own subscription.

Free trial

Pro and Business plans may include a free trial period. The length of any trial is shown on the subscription screen at the time of purchase.

Refunds

Refunds are handled by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play) according to their respective policies. Contact support if you need help requesting a refund.
