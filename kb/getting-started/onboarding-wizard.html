Getting Started

Onboarding Wizard

The first time you log in, StallVantage walks you through a 6-step setup wizard. You can skip any step and complete it later from Settings.

Steps

Step 1 — Business profile

Enter your business name, contact details, and default sales tax rate. The tax rate pre-fills every Point of Sale transaction but can be changed per sale.

Step 2 — Your craft

Choose the type of vendor you are (baker, candle maker, market gardener, etc.). This sets the language used throughout the app — for example, "recipes" vs. "products", "batches" vs. "units".

Step 3 — Markets & events

Add the market locations where you sell. You can add as many locations as you like and set recurring event schedules for each one.

Step 4 — Payment connections

Connect Square or Stripe if you want to take card payments at the stall. You can skip this and connect later from Settings → Integrations. Cash transactions always work without any connection.

Step 5 — Notifications

Allow push notifications so StallVantage can alert you when stock falls below your reorder thresholds.

Step 6 — Terms of Service

Review and accept the Terms of Service sections covering your account, billing, payment provider connections, and offline card acceptance.

Revisiting setup

All wizard settings are available permanently under Settings. To replay the wizard, go to Settings → Account → Redo Setup Wizard.
