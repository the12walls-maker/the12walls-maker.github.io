Kitchen & Production

Recipes & Cost of Goods (COGS)

The Recipes page is where you define what goes into each product, how much each ingredient costs, and what your profit margin looks like per batch.

Getting there

Tap Kitchen on the home screen, then tap Recipes.

Adding a recipe

  1. Tap Add Recipe.
  2. Enter the recipe name (e.g., "Sourdough Loaf").
  3. Add each ingredient:
  • Ingredient name
  • Quantity per batch
  • Unit (g, oz, cups, etc.)
  • Cost per unit — pulled from your Inventory if the ingredient is tracked there
  1. Tap Save.

The app calculates cost per batch and cost per unit automatically based on your ingredient costs.

Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)

Once you set a selling price for a recipe in Printables, StallVantage calculates:

  • Cost per batch — sum of all ingredient costs at the quantities you specified
  • Gross profit — selling price minus cost per batch
  • Profit margin (%) — gross profit as a percentage of the selling price

These figures update automatically whenever ingredient prices change in Inventory.

Editing ingredients

Tap any ingredient line to edit the quantity, unit, or cost. Tap the delete icon to remove an ingredient from the recipe.

Recipe image

Tap the camera icon on a recipe to add a photo from your camera roll. Images appear on recipe cards and printable labels.

How recipes connect to other features

Feature Connection
Inventory Ingredient costs flow in automatically
Prep List Active orders and event targets generate required batch quantities
Production Planner Batch production entries deduct from ingredient inventory
Printables Selling price set here feeds COGS calculation
Finances COGS data feeds the P&L statement
