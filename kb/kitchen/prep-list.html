Kitchen & Production

Prep List

The Prep List calculates exactly how many batches of each recipe you need to make, taking into account your active orders, upcoming market targets, and what you already have on hand.

Getting there

Tap Kitchen on the home screen, then tap Prep List.

How the prep list is calculated

StallVantage combines three sources to determine how much to make:

  1. Active customer orders — items from orders with Submitted or Ready status
  2. Event targets — quantities you have set for upcoming market events
  3. Finished goods on hand — units you have already made (set in Inventory → Finished Goods On Hand)

The formula is: batches needed = (ordered quantity + event target) − already on hand, rounded up to the nearest whole batch.

Reading the prep list

Each row shows a recipe with:

  • Needed — total units required across all orders and events
  • On hand — finished goods you have already made
  • To make — the net amount still to bake
  • Batches — how many recipe batches that translates to

Recording a completed batch

When you finish a batch:

  1. Tap Record Batch (or use Inventory → Record Batch).
  2. Select the recipe and enter the number of batches completed.
  3. Tap Record.

StallVantage deducts the used ingredients from Inventory and logs the production run in the Batch Log.

Event targets

Set how many units you want to bring to a specific market event from the Events Calendar. These targets flow into the prep list automatically.

Marking items prepared

On individual order cards (in Orders), you can mark each line item as Prepared by tapping the checkbox next to it. This gives you a visual checklist as you pack orders.
