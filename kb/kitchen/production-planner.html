Kitchen & Production

Production Planner

The Production Planner gives you a detailed view of what ingredients are allocated to your upcoming production runs and lets you track batch entries over time.

Getting there

Tap Kitchen on the home screen, then tap Production Planner.

What the planner shows

The planner lists every recipe that has quantities in the prep list and shows:

  • The ingredients required for the planned batches
  • Current stock of each ingredient from Inventory
  • Whether you have enough of each ingredient to complete the planned production

Rows highlighted in amber or red indicate ingredients where current stock may not be sufficient.

Ingredient allocation view

For each recipe, expand the row to see the full ingredient breakdown:

  • Required — total amount needed for the planned batches
  • In stock — current inventory quantity
  • Shortfall — any gap that will need to be sourced before baking

Batch production entries

Every time you record a batch (from here or from Inventory), an entry is logged in the Batch Log with:

  • Date and time
  • Recipe name
  • Quantity of batches completed
  • Source (Manual entry or from Prep List)

The Batch Log is accessible at the bottom of both the Production Planner and the Inventory page.

Using the planner on market day

The Production Planner is designed for the days before a market. Check it after finalizing your orders and event targets to ensure your pantry is stocked before you start baking.
