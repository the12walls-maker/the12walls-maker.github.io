Compliance

Compliance Center

The Compliance Center helps you maintain food safety records, track ingredient lot numbers, and prepare for inspections.

Getting there

Tap Kitchen on the home screen, then tap Compliance.

What the Compliance Center includes

Regulatory checklists

Pre-built checklists for common cottage food and farmers market regulatory requirements. Check off items as you complete them. Checklists are organized by category (labeling, storage, handling, etc.).

To add a custom checklist item, tap Add Item within any checklist section.

Temperature logs

Record fridge, freezer, and hot-hold temperatures to demonstrate safe food handling.

  1. Tap Add Temperature Log.
  2. Select the location (e.g., "Walk-in cooler", "Display fridge").
  3. Enter the temperature and unit (°F or °C).
  4. Add any notes.
  5. Tap Save.

Logs are sorted by date and can be exported to CSV.

Cleaning logs

Track when equipment, surfaces, and utensils were last cleaned and sanitized.

  1. Tap Add Cleaning Log.
  2. Enter what was cleaned, who cleaned it, the date/time, and any chemicals or procedures used.
  3. Tap Save.

Ingredient lot tracking

For full traceability, log the lot number and supplier for each ingredient batch you use. This is especially important for cottage food licensees who must be able to recall specific production runs.

  1. Tap Add Ingredient Lot.
  2. Select the ingredient from your inventory.
  3. Enter the lot number, supplier, and date received.
  4. Tap Save.

Ingredient safety profiles

For each ingredient, you can record allergen information, handling requirements, and any special notes for staff.

  1. Tap the ingredient name in the Ingredient Safety Profiles section.
  2. Toggle applicable allergens (gluten, dairy, eggs, nuts, etc.).
  3. Add handling notes.
  4. Tap Save.

Production traceability

Each batch production entry from your Inventory automatically appears here, giving you a timestamped record of what was made and when.

Exporting compliance records

Tap Export on any section to generate a CSV or PDF of the records for that section. Use these for your own files or to present to a health inspector.

Compliance documents

Upload PDF copies of your food handler certification, cottage food license, or other regulatory documents. Go to the Documents section and tap Upload Document.
