Settings

Settings Overview

The Settings page is organized into tabs covering your account, business profile, integrations, and team.

Getting there

Tap Settings in the bottom navigation bar (mobile) or the side navigation rail (tablet/desktop).

Tabs

Account

  • Profile — your name and email address
  • Password — change your password
  • Sign Out — sign out of the current session
  • Delete Account — permanently delete your account and all associated data (irreversible)
  • Redo Setup Wizard — replay the onboarding wizard
  • Subscription — view your current plan and upgrade or manage your subscription

Business

  • Business Profile — name, address, contact details, logo
  • Default Tax Rate — pre-fills the POS tax field
  • Checkout Mode — set your preferred Square and Stripe checkout mode (physical reader vs. Tap to Pay)
  • Expense Categories — add, rename, or delete expense categories
  • Forecast Settings — configure the forecasting model parameters
  • Notification Preferences — manage push notification settings
  • Weight Unit — choose whether to display weights in pounds or kilograms
  • Color Theme — customize the app's accent color
  • Vocation — your craft type (affects labels and terminology throughout the app)

Integrations

  • Square — connect or disconnect Square, manage reader settings
  • Stripe — connect or disconnect Stripe, scan for readers, set preferred reader
  • QuickBooks — connect QuickBooks Online for direct accounting export
  • Email (SMTP) — configure an outgoing email server for receipt emails

Team *(Business tier)*

  • View current team members
  • Invite new members
  • Change roles or remove members

Notification preferences

Go to Settings → Business → Notification Preferences to control which events trigger push notifications:

  • Low-stock alerts
  • New order submitted via shareable form
  • Order-ready reminders
  • Offline charge reconnection reminders
