General FAQ

What is StallVantage?

StallVantage is an all-in-one vendor operations app for farmers market sellers, pop-up vendors, and cottage food producers. It handles sales, orders, inventory, recipes, finances, and compliance in one place.

Does StallVantage work offline?

Yes. The Point of Sale, order management, recipes, prep list, and inventory all function without an internet connection. Data syncs to the cloud automatically when connectivity is restored.

Card payments have a 24-hour offline window — see Offline payments for details.

What platforms does StallVantage run on?

  • iPhone and iPad (iOS 16 or later)
  • Android phones and tablets
  • Windows desktop

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Offline card charges are stored with AES-256 encryption on your device. Your account is protected by email/password authentication. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Account → Delete Account.

Does StallVantage share my data with third parties?

StallVantage connects to Square, Stripe, and QuickBooks only when you explicitly authorize those integrations. No data is sold to third parties. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I use StallVantage without connecting Square or Stripe?

Yes. Cash sales work fully without any payment integration. You can also record card payments as cash and manage the provider payment separately.

How do I get my data out of StallVantage?

  • Sales and orders: export to CSV from Finances → Accounting.
  • Recipes: print or share as PDF from Printables.
  • Expenses: export to CSV from Finances → Expenses.
  • Tax data: export to CSV from Finances → Tax.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

You continue to have access to paid features until your current billing period ends. After that, your account reverts to the Free tier. Your data is not deleted — it is simply no longer accessible through gated features.

I switched devices. Will my data carry over?

Yes. Sign in with the same account on your new device and all your data syncs from the cloud automatically.

Can I use StallVantage for a business that is not a bakery?

Yes. The app adapts its language based on your craft. During onboarding (or in Settings → Vocation), choose from baker, candle maker, market gardener, artisan, and other options. Feature names update to match your business type.

How do I contact support?

Visit our support page at stallvantage.com/support or email support@stallvantage.com.
