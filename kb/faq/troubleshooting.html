Troubleshooting FAQ

The app is not loading data

  1. Check your internet connection.
  2. Pull down to refresh on any list screen.
  3. Sign out and sign back in.
  4. If the issue persists, check our status page (if provided) or contact support.

A sale was charged to the customer's card but I do not see it in the app

This can happen if the internet connection dropped after the card charge was approved but before the sale record was saved. Look for a Retry — Save Sale Record button on the POS Confirm Sale screen (if you have not navigated away). If you have already navigated away, contact support with the payment ID from the Sale Complete screen.

Offline sales are not syncing

  1. Ensure the device is back online (Wi-Fi or cellular).
  2. Open the app — sync happens automatically.
  3. Check Sales & Orders for transactions with a "Pending" status badge.
  4. If a pending sale is more than 24 hours old, the card charge may have been declined by the provider. Contact your payment provider (Square or Stripe) to confirm.

My Square / Stripe connection shows "Needs updated permissions"

Go to Settings → Integrations → Square (or Stripe) and tap Reconnect. This takes you through the authorization flow again. Your historical data is not affected.

Card reader is not appearing in the Choose Provider dialog

  • Make sure the reader is powered on and paired/connected to your device.
  • For Square: open the Square app once to ensure the reader is initialized, then return to StallVantage.
  • For Stripe: go to Settings → Integrations → Stripe → Scan for Readers and run a fresh scan.
  • Ensure Bluetooth or Wi-Fi is enabled on your device.

I cannot see the Analytics or Forecast pages

These are Pro / Business features. Go to Settings → Subscription to check your current plan. If you have already subscribed, try signing out and back in to refresh your entitlements.

Push notifications are not arriving

  1. Check notification permissions: Settings (device) → StallVantage → Notifications — ensure notifications are allowed.
  2. In StallVantage, go to Settings → Notifications and confirm the relevant notification types are enabled.
  3. Low-stock alerts only fire when an inventory update triggers a threshold breach — they do not fire in real time if you are looking at the screen.

The app is asking me to redo onboarding after I log in on a new device

This is expected if this is the first login on that device. The onboarding wizard runs once per user account per device. Complete it — it only takes a few minutes — or tap Skip on each step to go straight to the app.

A recipe's cost looks wrong

  • Check that all ingredient prices in Inventory are up to date.
  • Verify the ingredient quantities in the recipe match your actual recipe.
  • If you recently changed a unit (e.g., from oz to g), re-enter the cost per unit in Inventory to make sure the unit matches.

My prep list is showing more than I need to bake

The prep list accounts for active orders AND event targets. Check the Finished Goods On Hand section in Inventory — if you have already baked some items but have not recorded them there, the prep list will not know about them.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data and cannot be undone. Subscription cancellation is separate — cancel via App Store or Google Play before deleting your account.
