Billing FAQ

What plans are available?

Plan Price Key feature
Free $0 Core POS, recipes, inventory (with ads)
Pro $9.99/month Everything Free + analytics, P&L, Square/Stripe sync
Business $24.99/month or $249/year Everything Pro + team management
Ad Removal One-time purchase Removes ads from the Free plan

How do I upgrade?

Go to Settings → Subscription and tap the plan you want. The purchase is handled by the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android).

Can I cancel any time?

Yes. Cancel through your App Store or Google Play subscription settings. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.

Do I get a refund if I cancel?

Refunds are subject to Apple's or Google's refund policies. Apple generally offers refunds within 90 days of purchase. For refund requests, contact Apple or Google directly, or reach out to our support team and we can help guide you.

How does the Business plan work for team members?

Your team members automatically receive the same access level as your subscription. If you are on Business, they get Business-level access at no extra charge. They do not need to purchase their own subscription.

What happens if the workspace owner's subscription lapses?

All team members' access reverts to Free tier until the owner renews.

Is there a free trial?

Pro and Business plans may offer a trial period. The duration, if available, is shown on the subscription screen when you tap the plan.

Can I switch from monthly to annual billing?

Yes. Go to Settings → Subscription and select the annual Business plan. The change takes effect at your next renewal.

I was charged but I do not have Pro access. What do I do?

First, try signing out and back in — the subscription status syncs on login. If the issue persists, contact support with your order confirmation number (from the App Store or Google Play).

How do I see my billing history?

Billing history is managed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). Check your subscription details in your device's account settings:

  • iOS: Settings → [your Apple ID] → Subscriptions
  • Android: Google Play → Profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions
