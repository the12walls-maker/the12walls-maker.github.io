Integrations

Square Sync

Connecting Square lets you take card payments through Square readers at your stall and syncs your transaction records with your Square seller account.

> Pro / Business feature. Square Sync requires an active Pro or Business subscription.

Connecting Square

  1. Go to Settings → Integrations → Square.
  2. Tap Connect Square.
  3. You will be redirected to the Square authorization page in your browser.
  4. Sign in to your Square account and grant the requested permissions.
  5. You are returned to StallVantage and the connection status changes to Connected.

The connection is authorized using OAuth — StallVantage never stores your Square password.

Background sync

Once connected, StallVantage syncs transaction data from your Square account approximately every 15 minutes in the background. This keeps your Sales history up to date even if you process a sale directly through the Square app or reader outside of StallVantage.

Taking payments with Square

See Taking a card sale for the full POS checkout flow.

Reader setup:

  1. Pair your Square card reader to your device via Bluetooth as described in the Square reader's setup guide.
  2. In StallVantage, go to Settings → Integrations → Square → Reader Settings to open the Square reader management interface.

Checkout mode

StallVantage supports two Square checkout modes:

Mode Description
Physical Reader Charges through a paired Square card reader (tap, insert, or swipe)
Tap to Pay Uses this device's NFC to accept contactless payments (requires supported device)

Change the mode in Sales & Orders → Order Forms → Business Profile → Square Checkout Mode.

Reconnecting or updating permissions

If your Square connection shows "Needs updated permissions", go to Settings → Integrations → Square and tap Reconnect Square to reauthorize.

Disconnecting Square

Go to Settings → Integrations → Square and tap Disconnect. This removes the OAuth link. Historical sales data recorded in StallVantage is not deleted.
