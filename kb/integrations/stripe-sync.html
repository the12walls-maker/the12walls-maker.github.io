Stripe Sync

Connecting Stripe lets you accept card payments through a Stripe Terminal reader and syncs your transactions with your Stripe account.

> Pro / Business feature. Stripe Sync requires an active Pro or Business subscription.

Connecting Stripe

  1. Go to Settings → Integrations → Stripe.
  2. Tap Connect Stripe.
  3. You will be redirected to the Stripe authorization page in your browser.
  4. Sign in to your Stripe account and complete the Stripe Connect authorization.
  5. You are returned to StallVantage and the connection status changes to Connected.

Setting up a Stripe Terminal reader

  1. Power on your Stripe Terminal reader and make sure it is on the same Wi-Fi network or is Bluetooth-paired to your device.
  2. In StallVantage, go to Settings → Integrations → Stripe → Scan for Readers.
  3. The app scans for nearby Stripe Terminal readers.
  4. Tap Connect next to the reader you want to use.
  5. The selected reader becomes the preferred reader for future checkouts.

Taking payments with Stripe

See Taking a card sale for the full POS checkout flow.

Checkout mode

Mode Description
Physical Reader Charges through a connected Stripe Terminal reader
Tap to Pay Uses this device's NFC to accept contactless payments (requires supported device)

Change the mode in Sales & Orders → Order Forms → Business Profile → Stripe Checkout Mode.

Managing preferred reader

The preferred reader is stored in your business profile. If you replace your reader or use a different device, go to Settings → Integrations → Stripe and scan again to set the new preferred reader.

Reconnecting Stripe

If your connection shows "Needs updated permissions", go to Settings → Integrations → Stripe and tap Reconnect to reauthorize.

Disconnecting Stripe

Go to Settings → Integrations → Stripe and tap Disconnect. Historical sales data in StallVantage is not deleted.
