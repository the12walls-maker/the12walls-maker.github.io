Markets, Events & Printables

Printables

The Printables page generates professional PDF documents you can print, share, or save — without leaving StallVantage.

Getting there

Tap Sales & Orders on the home screen, then tap Printables — or tap Printables from the Orders page action buttons.

Available templates

Order Summary

A formatted PDF for a single customer order. Includes customer details, all line items with quantities and prices, the order total, any notes, and your business branding.

Use it for: Packing slips, customer handouts, deposit confirmations.

Product Label

A compact label for a single product. Shows the product name, your business name, and any allergen or description text you add.

Use it for: Jar labels, bag stickers, display tags.

Packing List

A checklist-style PDF listing all items across multiple orders for a specific market event.

Use it for: Pre-packing your market bag, verifying what goes to which customer.

Market Day Report

A summary of a market event: total revenue, number of transactions, top-selling products, and any notes you add.

Use it for: End-of-day review, record keeping, sharing with your accountant.

Menu

A formatted list of your current products and prices, suitable for a table display or social media.

Use it for: A printed price board at your stall or a digital menu image.

Generating a PDF

  1. Select the template type.
  2. Choose the relevant event, order, or product from the selector that appears.
  3. Tap Generate PDF.
  4. The PDF opens using your device's built-in PDF viewer.
  5. From the viewer, use the Share button to print, AirDrop, email, or save to Files.

Business branding on PDFs

Your business name, logo, and contact details appear on all templates. Update them in Sales & Orders → Order Forms → Business Profile.
