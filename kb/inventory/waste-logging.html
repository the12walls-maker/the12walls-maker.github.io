Waste Logging

The Waste / Loss Log lets you record spoiled, over-baked, dropped, or otherwise lost ingredients so your inventory stays accurate and you can track losses over time.

Logging a waste event

  1. Go to Kitchen → Inventory.
  2. Find the ingredient row and tap the waste icon (broom/sweep icon).
  3. Enter:
  • Quantity wasted — in the ingredient's native unit
  • Reason (optional) — e.g., "Spoiled", "Over-baked", "Dropped"
  1. Tap Log Waste.

StallVantage deducts the wasted quantity from the ingredient's current stock immediately.

Viewing the waste log

Scroll down the Inventory page to the Waste / Loss Log section. Each entry shows:

  • Date
  • Ingredient
  • Quantity and unit
  • Reason

Deleting a waste record

Tap the delete icon on any waste log entry. This removes the record from the log but does not add the quantity back to inventory (since the material was already lost).

Why log waste?

  • Keeps your inventory quantities accurate for prep planning and COGS.
  • Over time, waste data can help you identify patterns — ingredients that spoil frequently or production processes that consistently result in loss.
