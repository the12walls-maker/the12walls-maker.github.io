Shopping List

The Shopping List automatically generates a list of ingredients you need to buy based on your prep plan and current stock levels.

> Pro / Business feature. The Shopping List requires an active Pro or Business subscription.

Getting there

Tap Kitchen on the home screen, then tap Shopping List.

How the list is generated

StallVantage compares what you need to bake (from the Prep List) against what you have in stock (from Inventory). Any shortfall appears as a line on the shopping list with the quantity you need to buy.

Items that are below their reorder threshold but not required by the current prep plan are also included as restocking recommendations.

Adding manual items

Tap Add Item to add a non-recipe item to the list — for example, packaging supplies, parchment paper, or a display item. Enter the item name, quantity, and an optional note.

Checking off items

Tap the checkbox next to any item to mark it as purchased. Checked items remain visible until you clear the list so you can review what you bought.

Clearing the list

Tap Clear Completed to remove all checked items. Tap Clear All to start fresh.

Exporting and sharing

Tap the Share icon to export the shopping list as text. Use this to share with a team member, paste into a notes app, or send to a supplier.
