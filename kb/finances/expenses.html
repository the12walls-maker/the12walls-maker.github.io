Finances

Expenses

The Expenses page lets you log business costs so they appear in your P&L statement.

> Pro / Business feature. Expense tracking requires an active Pro or Business subscription.

Getting there

Tap Finances on the home screen, then tap Expenses.

Adding an expense

  1. Tap Add Expense.
  2. Enter:
  • Amount — dollar value of the expense
  • Category — choose from your expense categories (see below)
  • Date
  • Description — a brief note about what the expense was for
  • Receipt (optional) — attach a photo of your receipt
  1. Tap Save.

Expense categories

Default categories include: Ingredients, Packaging, Equipment, Market Fees, Vehicle/Fuel, Marketing, Utilities, and Other.

To add or edit categories, go to Settings → Expense Categories.

Viewing and filtering expenses

The main Expenses list shows all entries sorted by date. Use the filter controls to narrow by:

  • Date range
  • Category
  • Amount range

Editing or deleting an expense

Tap any expense entry to open the editor. Tap Delete to remove it permanently.

How expenses affect the P&L

Every expense you log in this page appears in the Expenses line of the Profit & Loss statement for the matching period. Net Profit = Gross Profit − Total Expenses.
