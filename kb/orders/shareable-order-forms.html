Shareable Order Forms

StallVantage can generate a shareable link to your order form so customers can submit pre-orders from their own phone or computer — without needing the app.

Generating the link

  1. Go to Sales & Orders → Order Forms (Designer).
  2. Scroll to the Shareable Link section.
  3. Tap Copy Link or Share Link.
  4. Send the link via text, email, social media, or put it in your bio.

When a customer opens the link, they see the fields you have enabled in the Order Form Designer and can fill out and submit their order directly.

What happens when a customer submits

  • The order appears in your Orders list with a "Submitted" status.
  • It is automatically added to your Prep List.
  • If you have email notifications configured, you will receive a notification about the new order.

Deposit tracking

For paid deposits you have collected separately (e.g., through Venmo or PayPal), you can note the deposit amount and method when editing an order. This is displayed on the order card and on the PDF export for your records.

Customizing what customers see

All field visibility and required settings from the Order Form Designer apply to the shareable form. Hiding a field removes it from the public form too.
