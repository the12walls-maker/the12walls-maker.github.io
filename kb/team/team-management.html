Team Management

The Team feature lets you invite helpers, assistants, or market staff to your workspace so they can use StallVantage with appropriate permissions.

> Business tier feature. Team management requires an active Business subscription.

Roles

Role What they can do
Owner Full access to all features, settings, integrations, and billing
Manager Full access to operations (POS, orders, inventory, recipes, etc.) but cannot manage billing or team
Staff Access to POS and basic order lookup; no access to financials or settings

Inviting a team member

  1. Go to Settings → Team.
  2. Tap Invite Member.
  3. Choose the role for this person.
  4. Tap Generate Invite Code.
  5. Share the code with your team member via any messaging app.

The code is single-use and expires after a set period. If it expires before use, generate a new one.

Accepting an invitation

Your team member should:

  1. Download StallVantage and create an account (or sign in if they already have one).
  2. Go to Settings → Team → Join Workspace.
  3. Enter the invite code.
  4. Their account is now linked to your workspace.

Subscription for team members

Team members automatically receive the same Pro/Business-level access as the workspace owner. They do not need to purchase a subscription themselves. If the owner's subscription lapses, all team members revert to Free tier access.

Changing roles or removing members

Go to Settings → Team to see all current members. Tap a member's name to change their role or tap Remove to revoke their access. Removed members' accounts are not deleted — they just lose access to the workspace.

Multiple workspaces

A user can be a member of multiple workspaces. Switching between workspaces is done from the account menu. Each workspace has its own data.
