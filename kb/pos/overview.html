Point of Sale

Point of Sale — Overview

The Point of Sale (POS) screen lets you ring up sales at the stall, choose a payment method, and record every transaction — online or offline.

Getting there

Tap Sales & Orders on the home screen, then tap the Point of Sale card.

The checkout flow

The POS works in four sequential steps:

  1. Cart — add items and set discounts, tax, and tip
  2. Choose Payment — pick Cash, Card, or Split Tender
  3. Confirm Sale — review the total and authorize the payment
  4. Sale Complete — confirmation screen with optional email receipt

You can move backward at any step (except while a card charge is in progress).

Adding items to the cart

Items from your Printables menu appear as tap-to-add chips in the catalog section. Tap a chip to add one unit; tap it again to add another. You can also adjust quantities directly in the cart using the + / − buttons.

To sell something not on your menu (e.g., a one-off item), tap Custom Entry and enter a name, quantity, and price.

Sale source

Each sale is tagged to a source — either "Direct POS" or a specific market from your markets list. Use the Market / source dropdown to select the correct event before checking out. This keeps your sales history organized by event.

Totals panel

The Running Sale Numbers section lets you enter:

  • Order discount — a flat dollar amount deducted before tax
  • Tax rate — pre-filled from your business settings; can be overridden per sale
  • Tip — a flat dollar amount added to the total

Changes update the total in real time.

Receipt handling

  • Cash sales — StallVantage records the sale. You can optionally send an email receipt from the Sale Complete screen.
  • Square / Stripe card sales — receipts are sent automatically by the payment provider (Square or Stripe) through their own systems.
  • Offline card sales — receipts are not sent until the charge syncs online; use the email receipt option after the fact if needed.
