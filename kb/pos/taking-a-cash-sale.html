Taking a Cash Sale

Cash sales require no card reader and no internet connection. StallVantage calculates change and records the transaction instantly.

Steps

  1. Open Point of Sale from the Sales & Orders hub.
  2. Add items to the cart by tapping catalog chips or using Custom Entry.
  3. Adjust discount, tax rate, and tip as needed.
  4. Tap Continue to Payment.
  5. On the Choose Payment screen, tap Cash.
  6. In the Cash Tendered dialog, enter the amount the customer hands you.
  • The app calculates change due automatically.
  • The Continue button is disabled until the tendered amount covers the total.
  1. Tap Continue.
  2. Review the sale on the Confirm Sale screen, then tap Record Sale.
  3. The Sale Complete screen shows the order ID and total.

Sending an email receipt

From the Sale Complete screen, tap Email Receipt, enter the customer's name (optional) and email address, then tap Send. This requires your SMTP email settings to be configured in Settings → Notifications.

Correcting a mistake

If you notice an error before tapping Record Sale, tap Back to Payment or Back to Cart to correct the cart or payment amount. Once a sale is recorded it cannot be edited from the POS screen, but you can manage it from Sales & Orders → Orders.
