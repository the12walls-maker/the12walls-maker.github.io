Point of Sale

Taking a Card Sale

StallVantage supports card payments through Square and Stripe. You must connect at least one provider before taking card payments — see Square Sync or Stripe Sync.

Prerequisites

  • A connected Square or Stripe account (Settings → Integrations)
  • A compatible card reader paired to your device (or Tap to Pay enabled)

Steps

  1. Open Point of Sale and build the cart as normal.
  2. Tap Continue to Payment.
  3. On the Choose Payment screen, tap Card.
  4. In the Choose Card Provider dialog, select Square or Stripe.
  • A provider shows as "Ready" only when it is connected and the reader is available.
  • If a provider shows "Not connected", tap Manage Connections to go to Settings.
  1. Review the sale on the Confirm Sale screen.
  2. Tap Charge [amount] via [Provider].
  3. Present the customer's card or phone to the reader when prompted.
  • The reader handles tap, insert (chip), and swipe.
  • For Tap to Pay, hold the card or phone near this device when prompted.
  1. Once the charge is approved, StallVantage automatically records the sale and shows the Sale Complete screen.

Reader status badges

Badge Meaning
Ready Connected and available for checkout
Not connected Provider not linked — go to Settings
Needs updated permissions Re-authorization required — go to Settings
Unavailable Reader cannot be reached right now

If the charge fails

The app shows a Retry button. Check that the reader is powered on, paired, and within range, then try again. If the issue persists, switch to Cash for this transaction.

If the charge succeeds but the sale does not save

On rare occasions the card charge is approved but the internet connection drops before the sale record is written. The app shows a Retry — Save Sale Record button. Tap it as soon as connectivity returns. Do not close the POS screen until the sale is recorded.
