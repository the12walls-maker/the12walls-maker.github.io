Point of Sale

Split Tender

Split Tender lets you divide a single sale total across two payment methods — useful when a customer wants to pay part in cash and part by card, or across two different card providers.

How to use Split Tender

  1. Build the cart and tap Continue to Payment.
  2. On the Choose Payment screen, tap Split.
  3. The Split Tender sheet appears showing the full sale total.
  4. For the first payment:
  • Choose the method (Cash, Square, or Stripe) from the dropdown.
  • Enter the dollar amount to apply to this payment.
  1. The second payment (remainder) is calculated automatically.
  • Choose the method for the second payment.
  1. Tap Confirm Split.
  2. Review the split summary on the Confirm Sale screen, then tap Record Sale.

> Note: For card segments in a split, the card charge must be completed through the chosen provider's reader before the sale is recorded. The Record Sale button handles all segments at once.

Rules

  • Both payment amounts must be greater than zero.
  • You can mix Cash + Square, Cash + Stripe, or Square + Stripe.
  • The total of both segments always equals the exact sale total — the second segment is always the remainder.

Receipts for split payments

  • Cash segment — no automatic receipt; use Email Receipt from the Sale Complete screen if needed.
  • Square / Stripe segment — the provider sends a receipt for their portion through their own systems.
