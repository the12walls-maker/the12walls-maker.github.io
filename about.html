About the product

Built for vendors who have outgrown guesswork

Most market businesses start with spreadsheets, notes, and memory. That can work for a while, but it gets harder as inventory grows, orders increase, and every event starts to feel like a separate planning problem.

Before a market

Figure out quantities, check stock manually, and build a prep list from scattered notes.

During a market

Try to keep track of what is left, what sold fastest, and what still needs to be made later.

After a market

Reconstruct the day from receipts and memory to understand whether the event was actually worth it.

StallVantage is meant to make the operational side of the business feel as intentional as the craft itself. It is for the person who makes the product, loads the car, sets up the booth, talks to customers, and still needs a clear answer about what to restock and what to bring next time.
