About the product

Built for vendors who have outgrown guesswork

Most market businesses start with spreadsheets, notes, and memory. That can work for a while, but it gets harder as inventory grows, orders increase, and each event becomes its own planning problem.

Before a market

Check stock manually, estimate quantities, and build prep lists from scattered notes.

During a market

Try to keep track of what is left, what sold fastest, and what still needs to be made later.

After a market

Reconstruct the day from receipts and memory to figure out what was worth the time and booth fee.

StallVantage is meant to make the operational side of the business feel as intentional as the craft itself. It is for the person who makes the product, loads the vehicle, sets up the booth, talks to customers, and still needs a clear answer about what to restock and what to bring next time.
